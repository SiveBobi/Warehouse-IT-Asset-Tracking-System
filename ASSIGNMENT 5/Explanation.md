## Explanation

### Actors and Roles

- IT Administrator: Manages assets and system operations.
- Warehouse Worker: Uses equipment and reports faults.
- Warehouse Manager: Monitors reports and system performance.
- Maintenance Technician: Repairs and updates maintenance records.
- IT Support Team: Handles system alerts and issues.
- System Administrator: Maintains system access and security.

### Relationships

- Warehouse Worker initiates "Report Fault", which triggers "Receive Notifications".
- IT Administrator performs core system operations such as registering and updating assets.
- Maintenance Technician depends on asset search to locate devices before repair.

### Stakeholder Alignment

This diagram addresses stakeholder concerns by:
- Allowing workers to report faults quickly.
- Providing administrators full control over asset tracking.
- Enabling management to access reports for decision-making.

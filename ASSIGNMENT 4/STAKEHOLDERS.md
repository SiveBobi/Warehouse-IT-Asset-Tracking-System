# Stakeholder Analysis
Warehouse IT Asset Tracking System

## Stakeholder Analysis Table

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|--------------|------|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| IT Administrator | Manages all warehouse IT equipment including scanners, printers, and computers | Ability to track device location and status easily | Devices get lost or moved without record | Reduce equipment loss by 50% |
| Warehouse Workers | Use scanners, printers, and computers daily for operations | Need reliable equipment that works without interruption | Frequent device failures slow down work | Reduce downtime of devices |
| Warehouse Manager | Oversees warehouse productivity and operations | Needs visibility of equipment availability | Delays caused by faulty equipment | Improve operational efficiency |
| Maintenance Technicians | Responsible for repairing faulty equipment | Need access to repair history and asset information | No record of previous repairs | Faster troubleshooting time |
| IT Support Team | Provides technical support for hardware | Requires a system to manage support tickets and equipment issues | Difficult to track which devices need attention | Reduce response time to technical issues |
| Company Management | Makes decisions about asset investments and budgeting | Needs reports on asset usage and cost | Lack of accurate data about equipment usage | Better decision making through reports |
| System Administrator | Maintains the system infrastructure | Needs secure, reliable system performance | Manual monitoring of systems | System uptime above 99% |

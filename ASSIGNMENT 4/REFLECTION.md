# Reflection: Balancing Stakeholder Needs

During the development of the Warehouse IT Asset Tracking System requirements, several challenges were encountered when balancing the needs of different stakeholders.

One challenge was aligning operational needs with technical constraints. Warehouse workers require quick and reliable equipment to maintain productivity, while IT administrators need detailed tracking and reporting features. Ensuring the system remains simple for users while still providing advanced tracking capabilities required careful requirement design.

Another challenge involved balancing performance and security. While stakeholders want fast system responses, security requirements such as encryption and authentication add processing overhead. These aspects needed to be carefully balanced to ensure both security and performance.

Scalability was also an important factor. While the current warehouse environment may only require a limited number of users, the system must be designed to support future expansion.

Overall, the process highlighted the importance of understanding stakeholder priorities and translating them into clear, measurable system requirements.
